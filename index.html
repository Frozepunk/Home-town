About Bangalore

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital city of the Indian state of Karnataka. It is known as the "Silicon Valley of India" due to its role as the nation's leading information technology (IT) exporter. The city is also renowned for its pleasant climate, vibrant culture, and rich heritage.
